Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

At Open Waqf, we believe your data belongs to you. Our software architecture is built on a "Zero-Knowledge" principle, meaning we do not track who you are or what you do.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. We do not use third-party analytics trackers (like Google Analytics) or advertising pixels in our apps.

  • Wird: Dhikr counts and preferences stay on your device.
  • Tahara: Health data is stored locally in your browser.
  • Signer: Documents are processed entirely in browser memory.

2. Local Storage

Your data is stored in your device's Local Storage or IndexedDB. If you clear your browser cache or uninstall the app, this data may be deleted unless you have manually backed it up using the export features provided within the apps.

3. Server Logs

Our website is hosted on static hosting providers (GitHub Pages/Vercel). These providers may collect standard anonymous access logs (IP address, User Agent) for security monitoring. Open Waqf does not access or use this data for tracking users.
